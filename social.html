Phase 3: Big Emotions & Friends

The final phase of the toolkit brings everything together. Banjo learns how his internal calm and sensory awareness help him build deep connections with others.

Episodes 7–10 Focus:

  • Fear Regulation: Learning how to move forward even when the High Country feels "big and scary."
  • Frustration Tolerance: Staying patient when things don't go as planned with friends like Kozy and Kippie.
  • Social Attunement: Understanding others' feelings without needing words—a key part of Banjo’s journey.

The "Connection" Tool

Children discover that "noticing the small things" helps our own worries disappear and allows us to see how we can help a friend in need.
